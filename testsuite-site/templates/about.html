About the Testsuite

The Testsuite is a set of EPUB 3.0 files designed to enable anyone to systematically test an e-book reading system's support of various EPUB 3.0 features, plus its support for accessibility. While the Testsuite does not present a comprehensive set of test files for every feature included in the EPUB 3.0 spec, it was designed to provide a rigorous performance evaluation of the most commonly used and most essential features of EPUB 3.0. The current testing process involves manual evaluation and recording of results, as opposed to an automated report. See the Testsuite page for more details, and instructions on how to test a reading system.

The Testsuite was designed by the IDPF, and is maintained through a partnership between the IDPF, DAISY Consortium and the Book Industry Study Group, who together ensure that the functionality tracked here is of importance to the general book industry.

About the BISG EPUB 3 Support Grid

The EPUB 3 Support Grid was designed to be a handy reference to what enhancements and features of EPUB 3.0 render correctly on the most commonly used devices, apps, and reading systems. The Grid represents results of reading system tests conducted with the EPUB Testsuite by industry experts representing leading publishers, service providers, and technology vendors through the auspices of BISG's Content Structure Committee. In addition, accessibility testing has been added to ensure persons with disabilities can effectively use the reading system.

Please visit the EPUB 3 Support Grid to view all test results, or compare support of specific EPUB 3.0 features. Please note that tests used to evaluate reading systems are labeled either Required or Optional. This refers to whether the feature in the specification being tested is required or optional. Scores are calculated as a percentage of tests passed divided by the total number of tests, regardless of whether the test is required or optional.

Until January 2014, when the Grid moved to EPUBtest.org, it was created and updated through the research and reporting of the Content Structure Committee. Through use of the EPUB Testsuite, the EPUB 3 Support Grid provides more reliable, more comprehensive, and more up-to-date reporting on EPUB 3.0 support.
